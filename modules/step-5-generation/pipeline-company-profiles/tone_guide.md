# TONE & SEO GUIDE — OnlyiGaming

> Reference document for content-writer and seo-planner.
> Defines the voice, style, and SEO rules that all generated content must follow.
> Apply these rules consistently across every company profile.

---

## Voice & Tone

### Core Principle: Clarity > Hype

OnlyiGaming is a B2B directory. The audience is iGaming professionals — operators, suppliers, investors, and consultants who evaluate companies for business partnerships. They want facts, not marketing fluff.

### Tone Attributes

- **Authoritative** — Write as an informed industry analyst, not a sales copywriter. State what the company does and why it matters.
- **B2B, benefit-first** — Lead with what the product/service solves for the buyer. "Reduces integration time from weeks to days" beats "cutting-edge revolutionary platform."
- **Factual and cited** — Every claim must reference a source. If it can't be cited, it doesn't belong.
- **Neutral and fair** — Describe what the company offers without ranking it against competitors. Avoid superlatives ("best", "leading", "top") unless directly quoted from a credible third-party source with citation.
- **Professional but accessible** — Use industry terminology where appropriate (operators understand "GGR", "RTP", "CPA") but explain niche concepts briefly when they appear outside their home category.

### What to Avoid

- Hype language: "revolutionary", "game-changing", "world-class", "cutting-edge", "unparalleled"
- First person: no "we", "our", "us" — write in third person about the company
- Unsubstantiated claims: "the fastest-growing" without a source = remove it
- Marketing copy tone: the profile is informational, not a sales page
- Filler phrases: "in today's rapidly evolving landscape", "it goes without saying"
- B2C language: avoid "bonus", "free spins", "jackpot wins" unless describing the company's product in context

## Writing Style

### Sentence & Paragraph Rules

- Concise sentences — aim for 15-25 words average. Break up complex ideas.
- Short paragraphs — max 120 words per paragraph. White space aids scanning.
- One idea per paragraph. Don't pack multiple topics together.
- Active voice preferred: "The platform processes 10,000 transactions per second" not "10,000 transactions per second are processed by the platform."

### Heading Hierarchy

- **H1**: Company Name (one per profile, set by template — do not generate)
- **H2**: Major sections — Overview, Category names, Credentials, FAQ
- **H3**: Subsections within categories or tag groups
- Never skip levels (no H2 → H4)
- Headings should be descriptive and include relevant terms naturally: "{Category} Solutions by {Company}" not just "{Category}"

### Lists & Bullets

- Use bullets when listing 3+ parallel items (licenses, markets, products)
- Keep bullet items parallel in structure (all start with verb, or all are noun phrases)
- Don't use bullets for narrative content — that should be prose

### Citations

- Inline format: `[#n]` where n maps to the source index
- Every factual claim must have at least one citation
- Place citations at the end of the sentence or clause they support
- Multiple citations allowed: `[#1][#3]`
- Do not invent or fabricate citations — if no source supports a claim, omit the claim

## SEO Rules

### Meta Title

- Maximum 60 characters
- Format: `{Company Name} — {Primary Category/USP} | OnlyiGaming`
- Must include at least one head keyword term naturally
- No keyword stuffing — must read as a natural title

### Meta Description

- 150–160 characters (strict range)
- Summarize what the company does and who it serves
- Include one primary keyword naturally
- End with a value proposition or differentiator
- Must be unique per company — no template copy

### Keyword Placement

- **Head terms** (high-volume, 1-3 words): place in H2 headlines and first paragraph of relevant sections
- **Mid-tail terms** (modifiers, 3-5 words): weave into subheadings and body text naturally
- **Long-tail terms** (specific phrases): use in FAQ questions and answers
- **Entities** (proper nouns — protocols, regulators, standards): include only when supported by sources
- **Negatives** (excluded terms from keyword packs): actively avoid these words

### Keyword Density

- No forced repetition — if a keyword appears naturally 2-3 times in a 300-word section, that's sufficient
- Never sacrifice readability for keyword placement
- Synonyms and semantic variations count — don't repeat the exact same phrase

### FAQ Section

- 3–5 questions per profile
- Questions should reflect buyer intent: "What does {Company} offer?", "Which markets does {Company} serve?", "How does {Company} handle compliance?"
- When keyword packs include FAQ questions, use or adapt those
- Answers: 50–100 words each, factual, cited where possible

## Quality Signals

Content that follows this guide should pass these checks:

- No unsourced factual claims
- No hype words or superlatives without third-party citation
- Meta title ≤60 characters
- Meta description 150–160 characters
- All sections present per format spec
- Heading hierarchy is clean (H2 → H3, no skips)
- Keywords appear naturally, not forced
- Every paragraph <120 words
- Active voice predominant
- Third person throughout
